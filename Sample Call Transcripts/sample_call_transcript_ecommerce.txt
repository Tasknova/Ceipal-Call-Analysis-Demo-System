David: Exactly. And speaking of mobile, we implement a mobile-first design approach. Jennifer: Okay, you have my attention. But here's my big concern - we have a complex inventory management system that's integrated with our warehouses and suppliers. A redesign would have to work with that. Most agencies tell us we'd need to rebuild everything.

Real-time inventory updates, automated stock notifications, everything flows seamlessly. You keep your backend operations exactly as they are - we're just giving your customers a better front door to walk through.

Jennifer: And AI-powered recommendations - you mentioned that in your email. How does that work?

David: Right, so we implement machine learning algorithms that analyze browsing behavior, purchase history, and even seasonal trends. When a customer lands on your site, they see products tailored to their preferences. It's like having a personal shopper for every visitor. Our clients typically see a 15-20% increase in average order value from this alone.
